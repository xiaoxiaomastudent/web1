Consider a time or a season for travel to Florence.

STEP 2

Search your low-cost airlines.

There are many low-cost airlines in Europe. After you decide your travel plan and
you can immediately look for your ticket. Most discount airlines in Europe sell
their tickets exclusively over their website or over the phone. It is usually cheap
when you fly in the middle of a week, in the early morning or late night and make
you buy your ticket early and avoid weekend.

STEP 3

Strat to do the research and plan your schedule.

Choose the areas you will visit and look at the schedules for museums and other
attractions. Don't pack your schedule too tight, because there are too many tourists.
The way to save your time which is to buy your tickets online and register a time
and you can skip the line. Buy a Amici Degli Card that includes many museums,
gardens and so on. Bring your student ID and you will get the discount.

STEP 4

Think about to use public transportation.

Public transportation in Florence is widely used. Buy your tickets before you
board the bus, as the bus driver does not check tickets but you need to put your
ticket in "ticket validating" machine. You can buy tickets from the driver but they
cost more. Usually, people buy tickets at local tobacco shops and small stores
with "T" sign.
