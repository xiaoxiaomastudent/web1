La Specola Anatomical Collection
Museum of Natural History

The largest and best-known wax anatomical
collection, La Specola is the Museum of Natural
History in Florence and there are wonderful
taxidermy, including extinct specimens and a very
questionable-looking hippopotamus.

Fiesole

Fiesole, an ancient Etruscan city famed for
its healthy atmosphere and the presence of people
from all over the world. Nestled above a hillside
of olives and cypresses, Fiesole strikes the visitor
as both rather special and rich in culture.
